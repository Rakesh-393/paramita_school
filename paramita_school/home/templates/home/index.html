Introduction

Welcome to Paramita Heritage School, Karimnagar

Paramita Heritage School is one of Telangana's most respected CBSE-affiliated institutions, dedicated to nurturing well-rounded individuals who excel academically, socially, and morally.

Our holistic approach to education combines rigorous academics with co-curricular excellence, ensuring each student develops into a confident, compassionate, and capable global citizen ready to lead tomorrow's world.

About Us

Paramita Heritage — Igniting Potential, Inspiring Greatness

For over two decades, Paramita Heritage School has been the benchmark of quality education in Karimnagar. Our sprawling, state-of-the-art campus is equipped with modern laboratories, a rich library, performing arts centre, and world-class sports facilities.

We are proud of our consistent 100% CBSE board results, national-level sports champions, and alumni who are making their mark across India and the world.
